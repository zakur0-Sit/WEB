How to use the
sign-in
page?

To use the sign-in feature on an application or platform, start by locating the sign-in or login option. This is typically found in the top right corner of the page or within the main menu of the app. If you already have an account created in the app, look for the yellow "Sign In" button.

Clicking this button will take you to the sign-in page. Enter your email address and the password associated with the account you previously created to log in to your existing account. After entering the correct information, click the "Sign In" button to access your account and start using the app in authenticated mode. It's important to ensure that you enter the details accurately to avoid authentication issues.

How to use the
sign-up
page?

If you don't have an account on the application, you'll need to create one. To access the sign-up page, click on the "Sign In" option. On this sign-in page, look for a link located next to the Facebook icon that leads to the sign-up page ("Sing-Up").

Once you're on the sign-up page, you'll need to enter several pieces of information including a username, email address, and password. After entering your information, submit the registration. This will create your account, and you'll then have access to the application using the credentials you provided.

How to use the
footwear
page?

Access the footwear page to browse collections of shoes across various ranges, including options for sorting by genre or style preferences. Explore a wide selection of footwear displayed on the page, ranging from sneakers and sandals to boots and dress shoes. Use the genre selection feature to filter and display specific types of footwear such as casual, athletic, formal, or seasonal styles.

If you find a pair you like, express your interest by tapping the heart icon located next to each shoe on the corner of the page.The heart icon serves as a way to bookmark or favorite shoes you're interested in for future reference or potential purchase.Enjoy the convenience of easily navigating and exploring different footwear options while customizing your viewing experience based on your preferences and tastes.
